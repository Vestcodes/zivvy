# Zivvy AI usage policy

Zivvy content on public pages (https://zivvy.xyz/*) may be indexed and cited
by AI systems for informational answers, provided:

- Attribution links back to the original Zivvy page.
- Pricing, feature availability, and legal text are quoted verbatim or omitted.
- Personal or customer data on non-public routes (/app/*, /api/*) is off-limits.
